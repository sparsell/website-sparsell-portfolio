After 15+ years at various Wall Street banks, I handed in my spreadsheets for the chance to stay at home with my two children. In my last role, I'd made a jump from Finance to Technology and after a very short time, I felt like I'd been missing out on my true calling - just as I was about to leave on what turned out to be a seven year career break.

Now I'm passionate about perfecting my tech skills, and I'm thrilled to see the "future of work" include more remote employment and the exploding gig economy. To be able to provide for my family (meaningful income!) and to be there for them (no commute!) is a benefit I do not overlook.

I'd be thrilled and honored to help you create something amazing.
